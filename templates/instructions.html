How BookingBot Works

Set up once and the bot handles your court reservations automatically, every morning, six days out.

Setup & how to use

1

Enter your club login credentials

Add your club booking system username and password in the dashboard. These are used solely to log in and make the reservation on your behalf.

🔒 Your credentials are encrypted before storage and permanently deleted if you close your account.
2

Connect your calendar in the dashboard

BookingBot reads from one dedicated calendar only. You can create a new calendar in Google and name it TennisBookingBot, or let the bot create it for you automatically during the connection step. If you prefer to use an existing calendar with a different name, you can specify that in the dashboard, just ensure the name matches.

3

Add your partners (optional)

Managing partners saves you from typing out a person's full name every time you add an event to the calendar. In the Manage Partners section, enter their full directory name and a short nickname. You can edit nicknames and save them by clicking on the floppy disk icon. Alternatively, if your partner uses the same email for the club or this bot, you can simply add them as a guest in the calendar attendee list.

4

Create calendar events for your desired bookings

Add an event to your TennisBookingBot calendar at the exact time you want to play. The bot checks the Guest list and event title to know who you are playing with. You can use full names, nicknames, or emails. BookingBot checks exactly six days in advance before 8:00 AM so ensure your events are on the calendar before that morning window opens.

5

The bot books for you at 8 AM sharp

Every morning before 8:00 AM, the bot checks whether you have any bookings scheduled for exactly six days from now. If it finds one, it logs into the club system and attempts to reserve a court at that time. No action needed from you.
