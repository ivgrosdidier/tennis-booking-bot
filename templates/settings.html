Settings & Privacy

Understand how your data is used and manage your account.

Privacy policy — last updated March 26, 2026

1

Introduction

BookingBot is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how it handles your data, specifically concerning Google User Data.

2

Data collection and use

BookingBot requests access to your Google Calendar to provide automated booking services.

What we access: Your calendar list, to create a dedicated TennisBookingBot calendar and read/write events related to your tennis reservations.

Why we access it: To automatically book your desired courts when the booking window opens at 8 AM, and to update those events once booked.
3

Data storage and retention

BookingBot stores your OAuth refresh tokens in an encrypted database using industry-standard AES encryption, allowing the bot to manage your calendar while you are offline.

We do not scrape or store your personal calendar events on our servers. We only interact with the Google Calendar API in real-time to execute your requested tasks.
4

Data sharing and disclosure

BookingBot does not share, sell, or trade your Google user data with any third-party AI models, advertisers, or external entities. Your data is used solely to provide the functional features of the app.

5

User control

You may revoke the app's access to your Google account at any time via your Google Security Settings. Upon request or account deletion, we will permanently remove all encrypted tokens and personal data associated with your account.

Are you sure?
